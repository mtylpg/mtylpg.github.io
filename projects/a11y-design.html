My Role

This unlisted page you've been directed to has the beginnings of a standalone site I'm working on called A11Y.Design, where I'll be putting the concepts discussed in my Accessibility Education project into practice.

The general goal is to create a worksheet for individual designers or small teams to go through, pulling in the best accessibility exercises I've found online with a few of my own creation to create a streamlined primer on accessibility in the web and inclusive design. It's in side-project status right now, getting work on weekends, but should be live soon (at which point this url should point directly to it).
